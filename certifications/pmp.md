## Project Management Professional (PMP)®  
Status: Earned  
Issued: 2025  
Credential #4098906

### Overview  
The Project Management Professional (PMP)® certification validates advanced knowledge and experience in leading and directing projects across diverse environments. It demonstrates the ability to manage people, processes, and business priorities using predictive, agile, and hybrid project management approaches.

### Skills Demonstrated  
Project planning, execution, and monitoring  
Stakeholder engagement and communication  
Risk management and issue resolution  
Agile, hybrid, and predictive delivery methods  
Scope, schedule, cost, and quality management  
Team leadership and performance management  
Governance and compliance alignment  

### Tools & Concepts  
PMBOK® Guide (7th Edition) principles  
Agile and hybrid frameworks (Scrum, Kanban)  
Work breakdown structures (WBS) and roadmaps  
Risk registers and issue logs  
KPIs, metrics, and performance reporting  
Change management and benefits realization  

### Related Coursework / Experience  
Leading cross-functional teams and initiatives  
Developing project plans, schedules, and risk logs  
Managing competing priorities in fast-paced environments  
Supporting operational readiness and continuous improvement efforts  

### Why This Matters  
PMP strengthens my ability to lead complex initiatives by bridging strategy, execution, and operations. Combined with my cybersecurity and technical background, it enables me to deliver secure, compliant, and resilient solutions while effectively managing stakeholders, risk, and change.
